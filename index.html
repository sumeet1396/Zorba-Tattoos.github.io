WELCOME TO
ZORBA TATTOOS

Located in Virar(W) Maharashtra, Zorba Tattoos provides a place where people can enjoy art and be inspired to create their own personal style in body expression. We offer custom designed body art as well as a full range of professional piercing.
All Hygiene & Standard equipment's are strictly Maintained for safety of both customer's & artist. Check our website and Facebook page regularly to stay informed about upcoming art shows and events.




Artist



GAURAV

Gaurav started The Studio in 2013. Art is his favorite pass time on this earth. He loves tattooing very colorful, organic images or text-heavy, graphic images. Birds, animals, flowers, trees, and old beat up antique items are among his favorites. He also loves illustration and painting. And will do custom tattoos or artwork.
